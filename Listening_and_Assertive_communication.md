# Listening and Assertive Communication

## What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening?

let the other person finish speaking before responding  
Do not interrupt the other person while speaking  
Try to fully hear and understand the speaker  
Develop body language to show that you are understanding what others are speaking  
Do not think too much while listening  
Try to avoid distractions and focus on the conversation  
Maintain eye contact and positive body language to show interest  

---

## According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?

Listen carefully to the speaker.  
Understand the speaker's feelings and message.  
Repeat the message in your own words.  
Check if your understanding is correct.  
Observe body language and tone.  
Ask questions if you get stuck anywhere.  

---

## What are the obstacles in your listening process?

I forget some points after the conversation.  
Sometimes I interrupt others while speaking.  
I lose focus during long conversations.  
Sometimes my mobile phone distracts me while listening.  

---

## What can you do to improve your listening?

Try not to get distracted while the speaker is speaking.  
Try to avoid using my mobile phone during conversations.  
I will try not to interrupt others.  
I will take notes for important discussions.  
I will ask questions if I don't understand properly.  

---

## When do you switch to Passive communication style in your day to day life?

I become quiet when talking to elders or teachers.  
I avoid arguments with others.  
Sometimes I do not share my opinion in groups.  
I say “okay” even when I do not fully agree.  
I become passive when I feel less confident.  

---

## When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?

I become aggressive when I get very angry.  
While I am speaking, if someone is not listening to my words, then I become aggressive.  

---

## When do you switch into Passive Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?

I avoid talking directly when I do not like something.  
Sometimes I show my anger indirectly instead of speaking clearly.  
I stop talking after small arguments.  

---

## How can you make your communication assertive?

I will listen carefully before replying.  
Maintain eye contact while speaking.  
I will try to speak clearly so everyone can understand.  
Try to understand your feelings and respect others.  
Stay calm during heated arguments.
